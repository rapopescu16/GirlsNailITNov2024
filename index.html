Our Mission

Girls Nail IT is dedicated to inspiring and empowering young girls to explore careers in technology. We provide resources, events, and a supportive community for girls to learn, grow, and excel in the IT world.

About Us

Founded with the vision to close the gender gap in technology, we are passionate about creating opportunities for girls to learn coding, network, and gain the confidence they need to thrive in tech fields.
